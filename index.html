What We Do

Services Built for
Government & Commercial Clients

From medical supply procurement to technology solutions — end-to-end support for state and federal government clients.

Medical Supplies

Procurement and distribution of medical supplies and equipment for state agencies, healthcare facilities, and commercial clients.

Government Contracting

Full-service support for California state and federal contract solicitations — bid prep, CUF compliance, DVBE documentation, SAM.gov opportunities, federal RFPs/RFQs, SDVOSB set-asides, and sole-source awards.

Supply Chain & Logistics

Efficient drop-ship and direct delivery logistics coordinated with established suppliers for timely contract fulfillment.

Procurement Consulting

Advisory services helping agencies and prime contractors maximize DVBE participation under CA procurement rules and SDVOSB participation against the federal 3% goal — including small business subcontracting plans and agency compliance.

Vendor Sourcing

Strategic vendor identification and qualification — connecting agencies with compliant, cost-effective suppliers.

Compliance Support

Assistance with SABRC certifications, technology disclosures, Darfur Act filings, FAR/DFARS clauses, SAM.gov annual renewals, and federal representations & certifications.

Technology & Automation

Systems implementation, CRM setup, and process efficiency solutions that streamline operations and scale revenue.

Sales Strategy

Revenue optimization and sales growth consulting backed by $20M+ in proven annual sales leadership experience.
